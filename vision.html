Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that prepares students for global success.

Target International School envisions being recognized globally as a center of educational excellence that nurtures intellectual curiosity, fosters innovation, and develops future leaders who will make a positive impact on the world.

We strive to create an environment where students develop the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in an increasingly interconnected and rapidly changing global landscape.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, high-quality education that prepares students for successful university admissions and future careers.

Our mission is to deliver a rigorous academic program with a focus on Business and IT education, complemented by a rich array of extracurricular activities that develop the whole student.

We are committed to creating a supportive learning community that celebrates diversity, encourages critical thinking, and instills a lifelong love of learning in our students.

Our Journey

Foundation

Target International School was established with a vision to provide quality education with a focus on Business and IT. Starting with just 50 students and 10 teachers, we laid the foundation for what would become a leading educational institution in Tashkent.

2005
Curriculum Development

We developed our specialized curriculum focusing on Business and IT education, integrating international standards with local educational requirements. This marked the beginning of our distinctive approach to education.

2008
Campus Expansion

Due to growing demand, we expanded our facilities and opened our second campus in Chilanzar. This expansion allowed us to accommodate more students and enhance our educational offerings.

2012
International Recognition

Target International School received accreditation from international educational organizations, recognizing our commitment to quality education. Our students began gaining admissions to prestigious universities worldwide.

2016
Technology Integration

We implemented state-of-the-art technology across all aspects of our educational program, including smart classrooms, computer labs, and digital learning platforms to enhance the learning experience.

2019
Multi-Campus Network

Target International School expanded to four campuses across Tashkent, creating a network of educational excellence that serves students from diverse neighborhoods while maintaining our high standards.

2023

Frequently Asked Questions

Our admissions process involves several steps:

  1. Submit an application form (available online or at our admissions office)
  2. Provide academic records from previous schools
  3. Complete an entrance assessment in English, Mathematics, and logical reasoning
  4. Attend an interview with the admissions committee
  5. Receive an admissions decision within two weeks

For more detailed information, please contact our admissions office.

Tuition fees vary by grade level and are reviewed annually. We offer several payment plans:

  • Annual payment (with a 5% discount)
  • Semester payments (two installments)
  • Quarterly payments (four installments)

For current fee information and detailed payment schedules, please contact our finance office or request our fee structure document.

Yes, Target International School offers merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid to qualified students. Scholarships are awarded based on academic excellence, leadership potential, and special talents in areas such as technology, business innovation, or community service.

Financial aid applications are reviewed confidentially by our financial aid committee. Interested families should request a financial aid application from our admissions office.

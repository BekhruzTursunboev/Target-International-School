Our Story

Established in 2005, Target International School has been a pioneer in providing quality education in Tashkent.

Target International School was founded with a vision to create an educational institution that combines the best of international education standards with a deep understanding of local culture and values. Over the years, we have grown from a small school with just a few classrooms to a comprehensive educational institution serving students from grades 1 through 11.

Our focus on Business and IT education has positioned us as a leading school in Uzbekistan, preparing students for successful careers in a rapidly evolving global economy. We take pride in our graduates who have gone on to study at prestigious universities worldwide and have built successful careers in various fields.

15+ Years of Excellence

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, high-quality education that develops students' intellectual, social, and personal skills, preparing them for successful university admissions and future careers in a global context.

We strive to create a learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning, while instilling values of integrity, respect, and responsibility.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We pursue the highest standards in all aspects of education.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and approaches to learning.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, ethics, and transparency in all our actions.
  • Respect: We value diversity and treat everyone with dignity and kindness.
  • Responsibility: We encourage accountability for one's actions and decisions.
  • Global Citizenship: We foster awareness and appreciation of different cultures and perspectives.

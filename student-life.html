Student Leadership

Student Council

Developing tomorrow's leaders today.

Our Student Council provides a platform for students to develop leadership skills, voice their opinions, and contribute to school governance. Council members are elected by their peers and work closely with faculty to organize events, address student concerns, and implement initiatives that enhance the school community.

Through their involvement in the Student Council, students learn valuable skills in leadership, teamwork, communication, and project management that prepare them for future roles in society.

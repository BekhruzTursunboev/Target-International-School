Academic Excellence

Our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire.

At Target International School, we offer a comprehensive educational program that combines the best of international standards with a deep understanding of local educational needs. Our curriculum is designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills while providing a strong foundation in core academic subjects.

With a focus on Business and IT education, we prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century global economy. Our graduates consistently gain admission to prestigious universities worldwide, a testament to the quality of education we provide.

IT Education

Preparing digital innovators for the future.

Our IT curriculum is designed to develop technical skills, computational thinking, and digital literacy. Students learn programming, web development, database management, and other essential IT skills through hands-on projects and real-world applications.

The program is structured to progress from basic computer literacy to advanced programming and system design, ensuring students develop a comprehensive understanding of information technology.

Key Focus Areas:
Programming

Python, Java, JavaScript, and other essential languages.

Web Development

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks.

Database Systems

SQL, database design, and data management.

Mobile Development

Creating applications for iOS and Android platforms.

Business Studies

Developing tomorrow's business leaders.

Our Business Studies program provides students with a solid foundation in business principles, entrepreneurship, and economics. Through case studies, simulations, and project-based learning, students develop analytical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills essential for success in the business world.

The curriculum covers a wide range of business disciplines, from marketing and finance to management and international business, preparing students for diverse career paths.

Key Focus Areas:
Finance & Accounting

Financial analysis, accounting principles, and investment strategies.

Marketing

Market research, branding, digital marketing, and consumer behavior.

Management

Leadership, organizational behavior, and strategic management.

Entrepreneurship

Business planning, innovation, and startup development.

Curriculum Overview

Elementary School (Grades 1-4)

Our elementary program focuses on building a strong foundation in core subjects while nurturing curiosity and a love for learning. Students develop essential literacy and numeracy skills through engaging, hands-on activities.

The curriculum integrates technology from an early age, introducing students to basic computer skills and digital literacy. Creative arts, physical education, and character development are also important components of our elementary program.

Core Subjects

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Uzbek Language
  • Computer Skills
  • Art & Music
  • Physical Education

Middle School (Grades 5-8)

Our middle school program builds on the elementary foundation while introducing more specialized subjects. Students begin to explore their interests and aptitudes through a broader range of courses.

The curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, research skills, and independent learning. Students are introduced to more advanced concepts in IT and business, preparing them for the specialized high school program.

Core Subjects

  • English Language & Literature
  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • History & Geography
  • Uzbek & Foreign Languages
  • Introduction to Programming
  • Business Fundamentals
  • Arts, Music & Physical Education

High School (Grades 9-11)

Our high school program offers specialized tracks in IT and Business, allowing students to focus on their areas of interest while maintaining a strong foundation in core academic subjects.

The curriculum is designed to prepare students for international university admissions, with dedicated IELTS and SAT preparation programs. Project-based learning, internships, and mentorship opportunities provide practical experience and real-world connections.

University Preparation

IELTS Preparation

Achieving excellence in English language proficiency.

Our comprehensive IELTS preparation program is designed to help students achieve the scores they need for admission to top universities worldwide. The program covers all four components of the IELTS exam: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

Led by experienced IELTS instructors, the program includes practice tests, personalized feedback, and targeted strategies for each section of the exam. Our students consistently achieve scores of 7.0 and above, meeting the requirements of the most prestigious universities.

SAT Preparation

Maximizing scores for U.S. university admissions.

Our SAT preparation program is tailored to help students excel in this critical standardized test required by most U.S. universities. The program covers all sections of the SAT: Evidence-Based Reading and Writing, Math, and the optional Essay.

Through diagnostic assessments, targeted instruction, and regular practice tests, we identify each student's strengths and areas for improvement. Our experienced instructors provide personalized strategies and techniques to maximize scores.
